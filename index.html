WELCOME TO FEATURE LEARNING PAKISTAN

Welcome to Feature Learning Institute, your gateway to excellence in education and professional certification right here in Pakistan. At Feature Learning Institute, we are dedicated to shaping the future of education by offering a diverse range of courses and certifications that empower individuals to thrive in their chosen fields.

As the educational landscape evolves, so do the demands of the modern world. That's why we've curated a comprehensive selection of programs that cater to the needs of students, professionals, and lifelong learners. Our institute serves as a platform where knowledge meets practicality, theory merges with application, and dreams transform into accomplishments.



Chairman's Message

The Jurisdiction of activities of BTPE is entire Pakistan. BTPE Islamabad conducts various Technical and Professional programs, with several institution courses under its progressive management, and developed a unique place all over Pakistan. The main objective of the BTPE is to monitor & evaluate the training through the physical visit and other means to ensure the quality of training. Arrange and conduct final Trade Testing & Certification for successful trainees. BTPE also provides assistance to the organization to develop and implement in-plant training programs to address the skill deficiencies of employees and organize seminars, workshops, and other programs to promote training and employment.

The introduction of the new educational system has opened doors to new experiences in the world of education and training. In this system, my board diploma has been transformed into a skill-based education center and completed through the RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) system. I can confidently say that this system is paving the way for a revolutionary change in the field of education.

The skill-based education system for my board diploma offers numerous advantages. Firstly, it is based on practical experience, which is a significant shift from traditional educational practices. By utilizing my practical experiences, this system enhances practical skills and competencies.
