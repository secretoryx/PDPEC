Introduction

WELCOME TO Pakistan Digital & Professional Educational Council

The Pakistan Digital & Professional Educational Council (PDPEC) has been constituted as statutory body under society registration Act (XXi of 1860), by the Government of Pakistan under National Training Ordinance – 1980 amended Ordinance – 2002 on the initiative of World Bank, ILO and Employers’ Federation of Pakistan, to make the Technical & Vocational Training Programs flexible, demand driven and cost effective with the maximum participation of employers.

The main social target of our Organization, Governments and of WHO should be the attainment by all the people of the world by the year 2050 of a level of health which would permit them to lead a socially and economically productive life. The basic aims of Trade Testing Council are to promote Technical / Health Education and to provide well-trained and highly Skilled Professionals to Society and to decrease unemployment graph level. It is basically an employer led autonomous organization functioning under National Training Board on the methodology of public & private partnership.

Students Review

The introduction of the new educational system has opened doors to new experiences in the world of education and training. In this system, my board diploma has been transformed into a skill-based education center and completed through the RPL (Recognition of Prior Learning) system. I can confidently say that this system is paving the way for a revolutionary change in the field of education.

The skill-based education system for my board diploma offers numerous advantages. Firstly, it is based on practical experience, which is a significant shift from traditional educational practices. By utilizing my practical experiences, this system enhances practical skills and competencies.

Druna Patia

Biology Student

Another notable benefit of the skill-based education system is its focus on industry relevance. The curriculum is designed to align with the current demands and requirements of the industry. This ensures that students gain knowledge and skills that are directly applicable to their chosen profession. It increases their employability and prepares them for real-world challenges.

Astron Brun

Science Student

The RPL system plays a crucial role in recognizing and validating the skills and knowledge acquired through practical experience. It provides individuals with an opportunity to showcase their expertise and receive formal recognition for their prior learning. This recognition holds significant value in the job market, as it demonstrates one's competence and proficiency in their field.

Paol Druva

Development Student

Furthermore, the skill-based education system encourages lifelong learning and continuous professional development. It emphasizes the importance of staying updated with industry advancements and equips individuals with the tools to adapt to evolving technologies and practices.
